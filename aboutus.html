BUSINESS BACKGROUND

TRIO CIS is fast food restaurant that was establish since January 2020 by Mr.Arif Daneil and his friends.

Our restaurant was specialized in fast food that served with three different types of cheese which are Mozarella, Cheddar and Cheese sauce. We choose or name as TRIO CIS because of these three perfect combination of cheese. We started as a street seller and it has been scaled widely when the cheese wave hit the market around May 2020. We now have 8 outlet throughtout Malaysia.

TRIO CIS only serve halal food in our outlet. So do not be hesitate to come and try our food!


Our mission is to provide an unforgetable experience to all the customer.

  • We want to improve the quality of the service and of the restaurant efficiency
  • Our goal is to gather our two passion:cuisine and new technology
